How it works

Suppose you are given any positive number N. To find the square root of N, do the following steps:

- Make an initial guess
In this algorithm the initial guess is always set as N/2 by default.

- Improve the guess
Apply the formula x1 = (x0 + N / x0) / 2. The number x1 is a better approximation to sqrt(N).

- Iterate until convergence
Apply the formula until the process converges. Convergence is achieved when the digits of xn+1 and xn agree to as many decimal places as you desire.
