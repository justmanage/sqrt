How it works

Newton’s method can also be used to find the inverse root of a number N. 
One way to write the function to zero out can be written as f(x)=1/X -N which derivative is equal to -1/x^2.
The substitution of this term in the equation of the first Newton's method, will give you as a result the recursive method.
