History of the algorithm

1 500 BC - Babylonian algortithm

The Babylonian method is one of the oldest methods of root approximation.
This method it is still a valuable tool for understanding both the development and functioning of root-taking methods. 

1680 AD - Newton's algorithm (1st method)

A common and easily used algorithm to find a good estimate to an equation's exact solution is Newton's Method, which was developed in the late 1600's by the English Mathematicians Sir Isaac Newton and Joseph Raphson
